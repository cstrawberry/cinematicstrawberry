Universe 00110000: Introduction

Welcome to Universe 00110000, a digital frontier where imagination and technology intertwine. This universe, while similar to our own, is a vast canvas for world-building, where each story contributes to a grander narrative. As we layer these narratives, a new reality emerges, parallel to our own yet boundless in its possibilities.

Nothing here is set in stone. This universe is dynamic, always changing. Ideas get revised the same way software does: you iterate, you improve, you ship a better version. The full history of those revisions is tracked openly on GitHub, so anyone can look through the commits and see how the universe has evolved over time.

The goal is straightforward and massive: build a simulated reality with the depth and complexity of the real world. Every world we create connects to the others, and the whole thing gets richer the more we add to it.

As you explore this universe, expect to uncover stories, challenge the very notion of reality, and witness the evolution of a cosmic simulation that grows more vibrant with each addition.
