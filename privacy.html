Privacy Policy

Information Collection

At Cinematic Strawberry, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party scripts.

To improve your browsing experience, this site may store a small amount of functional data, such as the page you came from and your reading position, locally in your browser. This data remains on your device, is not transmitted to us or any third party, and is used only to help you navigate the site.

Our website is hosted on Name.com as a static page. Name.com may collect limited technical data such as IP addresses, browser type, and referring pages. This information is standard for most web hosting. Any user information we access through Name.com administration tools is used solely for the purpose of website administration, security monitoring, and improving our services.

The limited technical data mentioned above is collected and managed by our hosting provider, Name.com. Visitors may have legal rights to access, erase, or object to the processing of this log data. Please follow the procedure in Name.com’s Privacy Policy to exercise any such rights.

Should you choose to contact us via email at contact@cinematicstrawberry.com, you will voluntarily provide us with your data (like your name and email address), which we use solely to respond to your inquiry. Your email is processed by our provider Titan, and as a result, your correspondence is processed and stored on their systems according to their Privacy Policy.

We delete correspondence from our email account when it is no longer needed. This action removes the email from our access, but please be aware that our email provider, Titan, manages its own data retention on its servers in accordance with their Privacy Policy, over which we have no control. Furthermore, this website is not directed at children, and we ask you to contact us if you believe a child has provided us with their data so we may remove it from our email account.

Email Newsletter

If you choose to subscribe to our newsletter, you voluntarily provide us with your email address so that we can send you occasional updates. We use this address only to send those updates and for no other purpose.

Our newsletter is operated by Kit, a newsletter service. When you subscribe, open, or click our emails, Kit may store and process your email address along with standard technical and engagement data, such as your IP address, approximate location, and email engagement, on its systems in accordance with its Data Processing Addendum and Privacy Policy. When you subscribe, you will receive an email asking you to confirm your subscription before any updates are sent.

You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in every email. For newsletter data access or deletion requests, follow Kit’s Privacy Policy.
